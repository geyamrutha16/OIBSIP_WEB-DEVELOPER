Biography

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and visionary who co-founded Apple, Pixar, and NeXT. He revolutionized the fields of personal computing, animation, and mobile devices with his innovative products and design philosophy. He is widely regarded as one of the most influential figures of the 21st century.
